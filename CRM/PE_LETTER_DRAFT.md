## 1. Engagement Model: Platform Aggregator
This engagement is governed by the principles of a **Platform Aggregator model**. YourPhotoCrew acts as a technology intermediary between photographers and customers. You are engaged as an independent entity and are not an employee of YourPhotoCrew.

## 2. Service Scope
The Photographer shall provide professional photography services for assigned events/deliveries as facilitated through the YourPhotoCrew CRM platform.

## 3. Revenue Share & Financial Terms (10/30/50 Model)
The revenue-share model is designed to reward productivity while covering platform maintenance and aggregation costs.

### A. Monthly Salary Benchmark
*   A "Salary Benchmark" is calculated based on days worked: **₹1000 × [Number of Days Worked]**.
*   This benchmark defines the tiers for commission calculation.

### B. Tiered Payout Tiers (Net Revenue Pool*)
*   **Tier 1 (Up to Benchmark):** You receive **90%** of the net revenue; Platform receives 10%.
*   **Tier 2 (Benchmark to 2x Benchmark):** You receive **70%** of the net revenue; Platform receives 30%.
*   **Tier 3 (Above 2x Benchmark):** You receive **50%** of the net revenue; Platform receives 50%.

*\*Net Revenue Pool = Gross Revenue - Rapido/Travel Charges - Penalties.*

## 4. Payment Settlements
### A. Customer-Paid Deliveries
*   For payments collected directly from customers, the Photographer must settle **30% upfront** via the CRM reconcile feature.
*   Final adjustment between the 30% upfront and your tiered share (90/70/50) will be settled monthly.

### B. Dealership/Platform-Paid Deliveries
*   Money collected directly by the Platform from dealerships will be credited to your account based on your tiered share.

## 5. Attendance & Leave Policy
To ensure reliability, the following leave policy applies:

*   **Standard Leave:** Must be applied for at least 24 hours prior to the shift start via the CRM.
*   **Emergency Leave:** Any leave applied less than 24 hours before shift start (10:00 AM for First Half / 2:00 PM for Second Half) is classified as Emergency Leave.
*   **Emergency Quota:** 6 half-days per month.
*   **Penalty:** Each emergency half-day beyond the quota incurs a **₹250 deduction** from the revenue pool.

## 6. Service Quality & Compliance
*   **Deliveries:** Must be marked "DONE" in the CRM only after successful upload and customer verification.
*   **Professional Conduct:** Photographers must adhere to dealership decorum and timing requirements.
